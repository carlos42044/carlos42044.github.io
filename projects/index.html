Projects

Loan It

Loan it is a simple loan amortization app for Android. The goal of this app is to educate people on how loans and loan payments work. Many people take out loans for cars, houses, and many other purchases without much knowledge of how they work!

By displaying the entire payment schedule of the loan, Lenders will be able to see just how much of their monthly payment goes towards interest and how much actually goes to paying of the loan itself.


Penguin Watchface
